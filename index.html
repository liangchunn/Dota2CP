How to use this app

Tap on the heroes that your enemies have chosen and click on 'Show Counterpicks' on the bottom right corner to show a list of good and bad heroes to pick.

To clear everything on the list, tap on 'Reset'.

To remove one hero from the list, simply click on the hero icon in the counterpicks page.
